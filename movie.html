My Hobby

Movie Watching 🎬

For me, cinema is a powerful gateway to the world, completely unrestricted by borders or language barriers. As an avid movie enthusiast, I have even learned English through film, using subtitles to unlock a global library of human experiences and diverse ways of life. Watching international cinema has expanded my worldview, offering deep political knowledge and insight into different cultures, while constantly serving as a source of immense happiness and positivity.

Movie-watching is more than just entertainment to me—it is a tool for personal growth and connection, perfectly capturing what filmmaker Bong Joon-ho famously said: "Once you overcome the one-inch-tall barrier of subtitles, you will be introduced to so many more amazing films."

Stories that don't just fill time—they shift perspectives
